Earn more with lending

Put your mSOL to work by depositing it into lending protocols like Kamino or marginFi. You’ll earn interest from borrowers while still collecting staking rewards—doubling your passive income stream without giving up ownership of your tokens.

Stack rewards through liquidity

Provide liquidity with mSOL by pairing it with other tokens on platforms like Orca, Meteora, or Raydium. You’ll earn trading fees in addition to the staking yield, making it a powerful strategy to maximize your returns—just be mindful of risks like impermanent loss and smart contract vulnerabilities.
