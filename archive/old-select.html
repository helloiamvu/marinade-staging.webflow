How it works

1. Stake Solana through Marinade Native
Connect your wallet and choose how much Solana to stake. Your funds stay in your wallet — Marinade handles delegation securely and non-custodially.
2. Toggle to “Select” mode
Simply switch the delegation strategy from Max Yield to Select. Your stake will be routed to a curated set of trusted validators.
3. Earn consistent rewards
Enjoy competitive APY with lower risk — powered by validators selected for reliability, ethics, and transparency.

How we pick trusted validators?

Marinade Select isn’t about the biggest names or the lowest fees — it’s about the right values. Marinade hand-picks validators with verified identity, a track record of reliability, and a clear commitment to Solana’s long-term health. No anonymous operators. No shady tactics. Just good actors running great infrastructure.
