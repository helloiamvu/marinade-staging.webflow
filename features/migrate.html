Your Questions,
Answered

Does Marinade Native take custody of the client's SOL at any point?

When staking through Marinade Native, your SOL remains under your institution’s or customer’s control at all times. Marinade Native does not take custody of the SOL being staked at any point, but by using Marinade Native, your institution is connected with the highest performing validators to stake your Solana at the most competitive staking rewards rate.

How do I become eligible?

You qualify by moving SOL that was already staked with an external validator before epoch 805 over to Marinade Native. Only existing stake accounts count — brand-new SOL or recently staked accounts won’t earn rewards in this campaign.

What’s the migration process?

You unstake your SOL from its current validator and then restake it using Marinade Native during the reward period. As soon as that SOL lands in Marinade Native, the campaign tracks it and starts vesting your MNDE rewards. There’s no extra form to fill out or claim button — the migration itself is the trigger.

How are the rewards calculated?

For every 1 SOL you migrate, you earn 10 MNDE. Rewards vest daily over the 3 months. If you unstake early, vesting stops on the day you leave. You keep the MNDE you’ve earned up to that point, but you won’t receive the remainder.

What’s the timeline for getting my MNDE?

Vesting begins the moment your SOL is staked in Marinade Native. Once the full three‑months period is complete, all the MNDE you’ve vested is going to be claimable. If you unstake before the period ends, you’ll only receive the amount that had vested by then. We'll be provide more info about this soon.

How is Marinade different than staking with a single validator?

When you stake directly with one validator, all your rewards depend on that one machine staying online and performing well. If it goes down or misses blocks, you earn less.Marinade Native spreads your SOL across hundreds of high-quality validators through our delegation strategy. That means steadier rewards, no need to research which validator is safest, and better decentralization for the Solana network.And because Marinade is non-custodial, your SOL stays in your own wallet the whole time — plus Marinade has completed SOC 2 Type I certification, proving our systems and processes meet strict security standards trusted by institutions.
