About MNDE

Marinade Finance launched its liquid staking solution, mSOL, on Solana mainnet in August 2021. The MNDE governance token followed in November 2021 as a fair-launch token with no ICO. On-chain DAO governance began in April 2022, empowering MNDE holders to vote on treasury management and protocol decisions. In July 2023, governance migrated to Realms, granting the DAO and Marinade Council authority over treasury and operational strategies.
